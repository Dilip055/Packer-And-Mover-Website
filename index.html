FAQ / Contact Us

Frequently Asked Questions

What Information should I tell movers on the first conversation?

  • Origin and destination locations,
  • What size house you are planning to move,
  • Approximately how many items you may have,
  • List of major items (bed, fridge, sofa, TV, AC, etc.),
  • Approx. date of moving,
  • Which floor you reside on (at both houses),
  • Whether lift is available or not in both buildings.

What Question should I ask movers before finalizing the deal?

  • What services are included in the quotation?
  • What packing material are you going to use?
  • How much time will it take to complete the moving process?
  • What will be my expected delivery date?
  • Will I get any tracking ID?
  • What if I change my moving date?
  • Are there any additional/hidden charges in the cost quote?
